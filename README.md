# Enterprise Risk Assessment Framework (ISO 31000)

This repository contains a self-learning **Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)** project focused on the practical application of an **Enterprise Risk Assessment Framework aligned with ISO 31000**.

The project is based on a fictional fintech organisation and is designed to demonstrate how enterprise risks are identified, assessed, treated, and monitored in a structured and realistic manner.

## âš™ï¸ Scope of Work

This project covers the full enterprise risk lifecycle, including:

- Identification of risks across strategic, operational, financial, compliance, cyber, reputational, and third-party domains
- Use of a **5-point likelihood and impact scale**
- Assessment of **inherent risk (before controls)** and **residual risk (after controls)**
- Development of a comprehensive **Risk Register**
- Mapping of **preventive and detective controls** through a Control Register
- Visualisation using **inherent and residual risk heat maps**
- Assignment of risk ownership and escalation mechanisms
- Use of **Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)** for ongoing monitoring
